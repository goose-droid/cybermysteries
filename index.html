Sherlock Holmes is one of the most recognized figures in liturature. He is a household name, and has been for over 100 years now. There have been countless adaptations of Arthur Conan Doyle's works featuring the detective, among which are hundreds of plays, movies, and TV shows. Less famous but equally as interesting are the many uses of Sherlock Holmes in other mediums. This site provides info on some of the ways Holmes has been called upon in the world of computing and video games, whether that be for education or entertainment.
